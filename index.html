Progression rules

  • Compounds: finish most sets with 1–2 reps in reserve.
  • Isolation work: final set may reach 0–1 RIR.
  • When all sets hit the top of the rep range at target RIR, add the smallest possible weight increment.
  • Rest 2–3 minutes on compounds and 60–90 seconds on isolations.
  • After week 6, halve the number of sets for one deload week.

Warm-up

5 minutes easy movement, then 2–4 progressively heavier ramp-up sets for the first compound. Warm-up sets should stay far from failure.
